Privacy Policy

App: PPRA PDE4 Exam Prep

Effective date: 24 June 2026

Overview

PPRA PDE4 Exam Prep is an educational exam preparation app for South African property practitioners preparing for the PDE4 examination. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not request or collect your location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, SMS messages, call logs, health information, or government identification documents.

Quiz Progress

The app may store quiz answers, progress, scores, and session history locally on your device. This helps you see your study progress. This local study data is not sold.

Purchases And Subscriptions

The app uses Google Play Billing and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, purchases, purchase history, and Pro access. These services may process purchase information, anonymous app user identifiers, device or other IDs, and app interaction events related to subscription status.

Google Play and RevenueCat process this data according to their own privacy and security practices. We use this information only to provide, restore, and manage paid access inside the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Purchase and subscription-related data may be processed by Google Play and RevenueCat because they provide the billing and subscription infrastructure used by the app.

Security

Subscription and purchase communications are handled over encrypted connections by Google Play, RevenueCat, and related platform services.

Children

The app is intended for adult property practitioners and PDE4 candidates. It is not designed for children.

Data Deletion

You can clear local quiz progress by clearing the app's storage on your device or uninstalling the app. For purchase/subscription-related data requests, contact us using the contact details below and we will help route the request where possible.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the developer at: liz@theendurancediaries.co.za

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version should always be available from the privacy policy link on Google Play.
